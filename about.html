Our Story

From a Bold Vision
to 1,000+ Clients

Merlins Group was founded with a single conviction: that businesses deserve technology partners who are as invested in their growth as they are. We operate at the intersection of data science and bold technical execution, delivering solutions in AI, automation, CRMs, application development, and migration projects that transform operations.

Having partnered with over 1,000 companies across construction, automotive, recruitment, healthcare, hospitality and beyond, we bring the rigour of a consultancy with the innovation of a specialist technology firm. Every engagement is architected to deliver measurable business impact.

Today, we're a full-service enterprise technology company with capabilities spanning AI and machine learning, process automation, CRM implementation, custom application development, data science, and system migration, all under one roof, all driven by results.

Recent News

Merlins Attended Parliament

Our co-founders had the privilege of attending a commemoration at British Parliament, recognising the courage and contribution of Commonwealth soldiers during the World Wars.

Hosted by Rt. Hon. Bob Blackman CBE MP and The Memorial Gates Foundation, the event brought together leaders including Lord Rami Ranger, senior British Army members, and community representatives sharing reflections on heritage, unity, and service.

It was a powerful reminder of the shared history that shapes our society and a real pleasure connecting with like-minded individuals passionate about making an impact.

AnyTax: Tax Filing Platform

We designed, developed, and launched AnyTax, a fully digital tax filing platform built to simplify self-assessment for freelancers, sole traders, and small businesses across the UK.

AnyTax streamlines the entire process from income entry to HMRC submission, with an intuitive interface that makes tax filing accessible to everyone, not just accountants.

Web App UX Design Tax Filing HMRC Integration
02

Making Tax Digital: MTD Compliance

With HMRC's Making Tax Digital mandate transforming how UK businesses handle VAT and income tax, we built a compliant MTD solution that bridges the gap between legacy accounting and digital-first tax reporting.

Our platform enables businesses to maintain digital records, submit quarterly updates, and stay fully compliant with HMRC's evolving requirements, all through a clean, modern interface.

MTD Compliance VAT Submissions Digital Records HMRC API
03

Project Management: Custom PM Tools

We developed bespoke project management applications tailored for teams that need more than off-the-shelf solutions. From task tracking and resource allocation to real-time collaboration and reporting dashboards.

Built for construction firms, agencies, and enterprise teams who need full visibility across projects, milestones, and team performance, without the bloat of generic PM software.
