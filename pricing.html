FAQ

Common Questions

What's included in the monthly retainer?

+

Everything listed under your chosen package. There are no hidden fees for strategy, reporting, or account management. The only additional cost is your actual ad spend budget, which goes directly to the platforms (Meta, Google, TikTok etc) and is completely separate from our management fee.

Are there any setup fees?

+

No. Onboarding, account setup, audit of your existing channels, and initial strategy development are all included at no extra cost. We invest in the first few weeks because it sets the foundation for everything that follows.

What contract length is required?

+

We recommend a minimum 3-month commitment to see meaningful results, but we operate on a rolling monthly basis after that. We do not lock clients into 12-month contracts. We earn your business every month.

Can I upgrade or change my package?

+

Absolutely. You can scale up, scale down, or swap services at any point with 30 days notice. Most of our clients start on Growth and move to Scale within the first 3-6 months as they see results.

How quickly can you start?

+

We can typically begin onboarding within 48 hours of signing. Growth clients are fully live within 1 week, Scale within 3 days, and Enterprise clients get same-day white-glove onboarding with a dedicated team.

Is ad spend included in the pricing?

+

No, ad spend is a separate budget that goes directly to the advertising platforms. Our pricing covers the strategy, creative, management, optimisation, and reporting of your campaigns. We will advise on the right ad spend level for your goals during our strategy call.
