Who We Are

The Creative Arm of
Merlins Group.

Merlins Agency is the dedicated marketing and creative division of Merlins Group. We handle everything from paid media campaigns and brand strategy to UGC production, social media management, and full-scale video production. Every campaign is built on data-driven insights, backed by the robust technical infrastructure of Merlins Group.

Having worked with over 1,000 companies across every industry, we bring a depth of experience that most agencies simply cannot match. Whether you are launching a new product, scaling an e-commerce brand, or repositioning an established business, Merlins Agency delivers creative marketing that moves the needle.
