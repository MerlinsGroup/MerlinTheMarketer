Frequently asked questions

What's included in the 30-day post-launch support?

All project packages include 30 days of bug fixes, minor adjustments, and support after launch. This doesn't include new features or significant changes, which would be scoped separately.

Can I upgrade my retainer plan?

Absolutely. You can upgrade at any time and we'll pro-rate the difference. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

Do unused retainer hours roll over?

Yes, depending on your plan. Starter hours don't roll over, Growth allows up to 10 hours, and Scale allows up to 20 hours to roll into the next month.

What's your payment structure?

Projects: 40% deposit, 40% at midpoint, 20% on completion. Retainers: Monthly in advance. We accept bank transfer and all major cards.

How quickly can you start?

Typically within 1-2 weeks of signing off. For urgent projects, we can sometimes accommodate faster starts—just ask.

Do you work with businesses outside the UK?

Yes, we work with clients globally. We're experienced in remote collaboration and can adapt to different time zones.
