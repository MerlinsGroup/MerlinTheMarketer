Merlins Group is an enterprise technology company trusted by the UK's leading organisations. From GSF and NHS to Greggs and beyond, we operate at the intersection of AI, automation, and scalable infrastructure, delivering technology solutions that transform operations and accelerate growth.

Having partnered with over 1,000 companies across every sector, we specialise in AI implementation, workflow automation, CRM solutions, custom application development, data science, and large-scale migration projects.
