How to Qualify

The Application Process

This programme is exclusively for registered Community Interest Companies. Here is how to apply.

1

Prove Your Status

Provide evidence that you are a registered CIC. This includes your CIC certificate, Companies House registration number, or CIC regulator confirmation. We verify every application to keep this programme exclusive.

2

Discovery Call

Book a call with our team so we can understand your mission, your goals, and how we can best support your organisation. This is about building a real partnership, not a transaction.

3

Get Onboarded

Once verified, you will be fully onboarded within two weeks. We set up your accounts, align on strategy, and start delivering results from day one.
