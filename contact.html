Common questions

Quick answers to help you get started.

What happens during the consultation?

We'll discuss your business goals, current challenges, and explore how we might help. It's a no-pressure conversation to see if we're a good fit for each other.

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on the scope. A website typically takes 4-8 weeks, while larger applications can take 3-6 months. We'll give you a clear timeline during our consultation.

Do you work with businesses outside London?

Absolutely. We work with clients across the UK and internationally. Most of our collaboration happens virtually, though we're happy to meet in person when needed.

What's your pricing structure?

We offer both project-based pricing and monthly retainers. Check our packages page for detailed pricing, or let's discuss during the consultation.

Can you help with an existing project?

Yes, we regularly take over or improve existing projects. We'll start with an audit to understand what you have and recommend the best path forward.

What if I'm not sure what I need?

That's completely fine—many clients come to us with a problem rather than a specific solution in mind. We'll help you figure out the best approach.
