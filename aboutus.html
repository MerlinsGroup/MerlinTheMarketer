Recent News

Merlins Attended Parliament

Our co-founders had the privilege of attending a commemoration at British Parliament, recognising the courage and contribution of Commonwealth soldiers during the World Wars.

Hosted by Rt. Hon. Bob Blackman CBE MP and The Memorial Gates Foundation, the event brought together leaders including Lord Rami Ranger, senior British Army members, and community representatives sharing reflections on heritage, unity, and service.

It was a powerful reminder of the shared history that shapes our society—and a real pleasure connecting with like-minded individuals passionate about making an impact.
